About Me

I am a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from CMR Technical Campus, Hyderabad. With a solid educational foundation, I have developed a strong interest in software development and data analysis, aspiring to contribute to innovative research and cutting-edge technology. My academic journey began with a Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Jyothishmathi Institute of Technology & Science, Karimnagar, where I gained a deeper understanding of electronics and communication systems.

Throughout my studies, I have acquired a diverse skill set that spans various programming languages, data analysis techniques, and hands-on experience with emerging technologies. I am proficient in Python, HTML/CSS, basic Java, and C language, enabling me to develop robust software solutions. My data analysis capabilities are enhanced by my knowledge of SQL and MS Excel, which I use to interpret and visualize complex data sets. Additionally, I have gained practical experience with Internet of Things (IoT) technology, which has further fueled my passion for integrating smart systems into everyday applications.

My commitment to continuous learning is reflected in my completion of several industry-recognized certifications.
